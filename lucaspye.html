About LucasPye BIO

Currently operating at Philadelphia CIC, LucasPye BIO is a pioneering Contract Development Manufacturing Organization (CDMO) dedicated to making biotherapeutic drugs more accessible and affordable.

Dr. Tia Lyles-Williams
Founder & CEO

A trailblazer in the Life Sciences world, Dr. Tia is committed to breaking barriers and building bridges in biotechnology. As a two-time life science start-up founder, she leads with a mission to make the biotech industry more inclusive while accelerating innovative drug development.
